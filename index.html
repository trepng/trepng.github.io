What is Baby Oil?

Baby oil is a gentle, mineral oil-based product primarily used for moisturizing and soothing the skin. Originally formulated for infants, its hypoallergenic properties make it suitable for people of all ages, effectively locking in moisture and preventing dryness. Beyond its moisturizing benefits, baby oil is also commonly used as a massage oil, providing a smooth glide that enhances relaxation. Additionally, it serves as an effective makeup remover and can help reduce friction in various applications. While generally safe, it’s important to perform a patch test for sensitivities and avoid using it on diaper rashes, as it may trap moisture. Overall, baby oil remains a versatile and affordable option for skincare and personal care routines.
